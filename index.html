INDULGE IN
ARTISAN TREATS

We pride ourselves on serving only the highest quality beverages and pastries. Cozy up with a delicious latte or keep it simple with a classic black coffee. If coffee’s not your cup of tea, we have plenty of that to choose from as well, whether you’re in the mood for a light oolong or a strong and sweet black tea. Not in the mood for caffeine? Try our new peppermint hot chocolate or spiced apple cider. If you’re looking for a snack, try one of our pastries such as a decadent cinnamon roll or zesty fruit tart, made fresh daily.

COME IN AND
STAY A WHILE

Get comfy while you sip on your coffee. Whether you want to relax on your own in a big cozy chair or catch up with your friends, you can feel at home at the Cozy Coffee Retreat. Take in the warm atmosphere of our lounge for an experience that’s guaranteed to make you want to come back time and time again.
Come on in during one of our music nights to listen to some live music. Experience great entertainment and discover new artists while you enjoy a warm drink and fresh baked dessert.

SUPPORT
LOCAL ARTISTS

We at the Cozy Coffee Retreat love to support and work with smaller businesses and artists. Every mug, plate, and bowl we use is handmade by local ceramists. If you can’t get enough of our beautiful, unique, and high quality ceramics, you’re in luck! You can buy some for yourself through our website. You can also check out our artist bio page to learn more about the talented ceramists we work with and where you can find more from them.
